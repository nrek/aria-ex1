# Ideas Backlog

Feature proposals, bug reports, and design ideas captured during Claude Code sessions via `/extract`. Reviewed during the knowledge audit as a separate section from promotion candidates.

## Purpose

ARIA captures observations about **what IS** (knowledge) and stages proposals about **what SHOULD BE different** (ideas). Observations promote into knowledge files; proposals route out to your external tracker (Linear, GitHub Issues, Jira, etc.).

**ARIA captures; your tracker schedules.** This file is staging only — ARIA does not replace your issue tracker.

## Format

Each entry includes the date, project tag, and the captured proposal.

```markdown
### YYYY-MM-DD — [project] — [short title]
**Type:** feature | bug | design | refactor | workflow
**Proposal:** What change is being proposed
**Motivation:** Why it would help (what gap or friction it addresses)
**Source:** Where in the conversation it came up (brief description)
```

## Disposition

During `/audit-knowledge`, ideas are surfaced in their own section without promotion suggestions. For each idea, you decide:

- **Accept** — copy to your external tracker, mark as routed in backlog, then clear
- **Reject** — clear from backlog with a one-line reason
- **Defer** — leave in backlog for a future review

Unlike the other backlogs, ideas **never promote to knowledge files** — they leave ARIA entirely (to a tracker) or get discarded.
